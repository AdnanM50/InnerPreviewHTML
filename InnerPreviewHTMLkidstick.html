Introducing kidstick. A cutting-edge kids activity management platform.

kidstick is a cutting-edge kids activity management platform that revolutionizes the way parents and caregivers manage their children's activities. The platform is designed to help parents and caregivers easily manage their children's activities, including sports, music, dance, and more. With kidstick, parents can easily find and book activities for their children, track their children's progress, and communicate with activity providers.

kidstick is a comprehensive solution that includes a web platform for parents and caregivers, a mobile app for children, and a mobile app for activity providers. The platform is designed to be easy to use and intuitive, making it easy for parents and caregivers to manage their children's activities.

  • Total Coaches: Indicates the number of coaches
  • Total Packages Sell: Tracks the number of packages sold
