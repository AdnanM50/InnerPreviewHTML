Introducing Gymstick Pro. Your Fitness Platform.

The ultimate web-based, multi-vendor SaaS module for seamless gym management. With dedicated dashboards for admins, trainers, members, vendors, and employees to manage the admin panel, Gymstick Pro empowers each role to efficiently handle gym activities, from membership and scheduling to personalized training plans. This fully dynamic and customizable platform is perfect for any fitness organization looking to simplify operations and deliver a top-notch experience. Transform how you manage your gym with Gymstick Pro, designed to meet the unique needs of modern fitness centers.

This Script Includes:

  1. A Complete Backend and Frontend
  2. Complete Admin Panel
  3. Complete Vendor Panel
  4. Complete Employee Panel
  5. Full Trainer Panel
  6. Full Member Panel

This comprehensive suite of tools enables businesses to streamline their operations and enhance overall efficiency.

Notably, customers who acquire this product from Code Canyon can readily embark on their entrepreneurial journey, as it facilitates a seamless purchase process and enables smooth establishment of their respective businesses.

Admin Dashboard Features

Admin Profile Settings

  • Basic Information: Name, Email, Phone Number
  • Change Password: Option to update password for security

Key Metrics Displayed

  • Total Members: Displays the total number of users
  • Total Trainers: Shows the total number of trainers
  • Total Groups: Indicates the number of groups created
  • Total Orders: Tracks the number of orders placed
  • Complete Orders: Shows the number of completed orders
  • Active Subscriptions: Displays total active subscriptions

Vendor Management

  • Product Management
  • Warehouse Management
  • Coupon Management
  • Order Management
  • Withdrawal Management

Member Management

  • Role-based Access Control
  • User Profile Management
  • Subscription Management
  • Password Management
  • Search and Filter: Find users by name,email or status

Trainer

  • Add Trainer: Register a new trainer
  • View Trainer: View details like name, phone, email
  • Delete Trainer: Remove a trainer permanently

Groups

  • Group Images: Upload images for each group
  • Group Facilities: Add or update facilities for each group
  • Assigned Trainer: Assign a trainer to a group
  • Group Status: Manage active or inactive status

Product Management

  • Category: Organize products for easy navigation
  • Products: Manage product details, price, SKU, etc.
  • Orders: Track order history and status
  • Coupon: Create and manage discount codes
  • Testimonial: Display customer feedback

Additional Features

  • Trainer Attendance System: Track trainer attendance
  • Marketing Module: Send emails to specific users, groups, subscribers, trainers, or employees
  • Schedule: Manage availability and time slots
  • Service: List services with icons and descriptions
  • Events: Manage event details, location, images
  • Newsletter: Manage subscriber emails
  • Gallery: Store images and videos
  • Blog: Publish articles and updates
  • Pricing Plan: Display pricing plans
  • Currency: Manage currency options
  • Payment Methods: Available payment options
