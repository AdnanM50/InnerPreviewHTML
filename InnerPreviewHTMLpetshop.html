Introducing Petstick. A cutting-edge pet shop & service management platform.

Petstick is a comprehensive solution that includes a web platform for pet shop owners and service providers. The platform is designed to be easy to use and intuitive, making it easy for pet owners to find the services they need and for pet service providers to manage their businesses.

The platform includes a user-friendly interface that allows pet owners to search for pet shops and services in this site, view detailed information about each service, and book appointments online. Pet service providers can use the platform to manage their services, schedule appointments, and track customer feedback.

This Script Includes:

  1. A Complete Backend and Frontend
  2. Complete Admin Panel
  3. Vendor Panel
  4. Employee Panel
  5. User Panel

This comprehensive suite of tools enables businesses to streamline their operations and enhance overall efficiency.
