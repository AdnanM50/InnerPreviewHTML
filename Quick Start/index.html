Welcome to Lawstick

Lawstick is a legal tech solution that provides a platform for lawyers to manage their clients, cases, and documents. It also allows clients to connect with lawyers, view their case details, and upload documents. The platform also includes a blog section where users can read legal articles and blogs. The platform is designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate, with a focus on providing a seamless experience for lawyers and clients .

This script includes:

1. A complete backend and frontend

2. Complete Admin Panel

3. Complete Business Website

4. Complete Trainer Panel

4. Complete User Panel
